STRATEGIC PLANNING

Based on more than 20 years of experience, our team of architects and business analysts offer organizing workshops with client deep involvement for assessing business needs, generating ideas and directions of improvements, discussing and planning next steps for implementation of set goals. Every step of this planning stage and following ones is fixed with appropriate documents which IP rights belong to the client.

ARCHITECTURAL PLANNING

When strategic goals are set, we propose following planning steps of architecture development, infrastructure planning, general technological and platform development stack. OPERATIONAL AND IMPLEMENTATION PLANNING

Working on these planning stage we offer production plan, development team structure, costs estimation plan, deployment and maintenance plans. Every planning stage or prepared document, concept paper or production plan, can be considered and ordered as a separate service unit.
